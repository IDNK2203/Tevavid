Always happy, enthusiastic and creative, is something to talk about Tevavid's Team
Tevavid started as a very small team of two dedicated to changing the idea of buildings in Edo state, Nigeria and West Africa at large Over the years this determination has led to the building of a larger team that has fetched experience and knowledge from experienced and seasoned architects around the world.
This mustering of knowledge has led to a seasoned team that is able to deliver whatever project that they are faced with.
From commissioning to completion, every project done at/with Tevavid is tackled with enthusiasm and attention, regardless of its size or scope. Regardless of their similarities, no two projects are treated the same because each one is meticulously examined by a team that includes architects, designers, and researchers.
