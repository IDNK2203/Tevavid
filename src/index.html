Being The People

Tevavid architectural designers (subsidiary of Ela Consults International), is a completely different and unique architectural designing company. Founded by Obi-Rapu Theophilus and Nosa-Ehima Magnus in 2017, we have created a design process that focuses on the end users and values the input of people. WE ARE PEOPLE ORIENTED.

Today we have several locations around Nigeria and around the world providing residential, commercial and restoration architecture, we also provide provide landscape design, interior design and green space designs from conceptualisation to completion of the construction.
